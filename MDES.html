Mobile Design

A pop-up store with a designed app!

For the MDES class, I conceptualized a pop-up store where guests can create and enjoy their own music using an app powered by advanced AI technology. The app guides users in music creation, making the event interactive and engaging.

I designed a comprehensive experience, including the customer journey, touchpoints, and visuals. The final project includes pictures of the pop-up store, app wireframes, user flows, and mockups.
