A pop-up store with a designed app!

For the class of MDES I created the idea of a pop-up store where guests can have a party
with their own created music using an app with advanced AI technology that guides the user for making music.

I had to create a total experience and design it the way I wanted to


In the pdf you can go through the experience that I worked out using the customer journey. It also comes with pictures of the worked out touchpoints, the pop-up store images and the app wireframe, user flow and mockups. In the end I was welcomed with a nice grade an inspired by my own idea!
