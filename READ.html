Research in Adaptivity

What if you see audio visually?


For Research in Adaptivity I researched and created a tool that visualizes audio. I did this by walking a path multiple times. In the results, I found that seeing audio can be useful for noticing sounds that your brain automatically ignores over time, like the sound of nature. Also, with visual audio, you could see loud noises like cars, which could be useful for deaf people to perceive potential dangers.
