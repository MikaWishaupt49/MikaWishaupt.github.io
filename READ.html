Research in Adaptivity

What if you see audio visually?

For Research in Adaptivity I researched and created a tool what if you could see audio visually. I did this through walking a path multiple times. In the results I found that seeing audio can be usefull to see sounds which your brain automatically ignores after time like the sound of the nature. Also with visual audio you could see loud noises like cars which could be usefull for deaf people so see danger
