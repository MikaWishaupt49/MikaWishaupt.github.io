Ravenstad

A game with the usage of AI, Processing and Arduino

A game that shows the horror of war for an innocent civilian that gets struck by explosions which became his eventual death.

The game is targeted to a Dutch audience to give an idea what war would look like if it all the sudden would happen in a simple Dutch town.

In this project I had to learn Unity from almost scratch with gave me insight in the engine but also how to program in C#.

With the game also comes a phone app. It was a proof of concept that when you enter the fire scene your phone goes of and shows your name and an attack in real life for extra immersion.
