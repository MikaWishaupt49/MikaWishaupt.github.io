Ravenstad

A serious game to show the horrors of war


A game that shows the horror of war for an innocent civilian that gets struck by explosions which became his eventual death. The game is targeted to a Dutch audience to give an idea what war would look like if it all of a sudden would happen in a simple Dutch town. In this project, I had to learn Unity from almost scratch which gave me insight into the engine but also how to program in C#.

With the game also comes a phone app. It was a proof of concept that when you enter the fire scene your phone goes off and shows your name and an attack in real life for extra immersion.
